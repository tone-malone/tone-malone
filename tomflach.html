About

Hello! I'm Tom Flach, a multi-talented individual passionate about music, programming, and game development. I create synth music under my Spotify profile Tom Flach and produce rock/metal/punk tracks as Eddy Woody.

My technical skills encompass computer literacy, programming, mechanical aptitude, and troubleshooting. I have developed several games, including the Frog game, Detective Dart - Lost Relics, and The Adventures of Captain Kane, each with unique gameplay and captivating narratives.

My career goal is to continually innovate and create immersive experiences through music and game development. Thank you for visiting my website, and I look forward to connecting with you!
